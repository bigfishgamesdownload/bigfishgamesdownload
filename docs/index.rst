Common Download and Installation Issues
=======================================

Download Won't Start
--------------------

If your download doesn't begin:

* Verify your internet connection.
* Restart your browser or app.
* Check available storage space.
* Try downloading again.

Installation Failed
-------------------

If installation doesn't complete:

* Restart your device.
* Download the installer again.
* Close other running applications.
* Verify your operating system meets the game's requirements.

Game Won't Launch
-----------------

If the game doesn't open:

* Restart your computer or mobile device.
* Check for available game updates.
* Update your operating system.
* Reinstall the game if necessary.

Cannot Find Purchased Game
--------------------------

If your purchased game isn't listed:

* Confirm you're signed in with the correct account.
* Refresh your game library.
* Verify your purchase history.
* Allow a few minutes if the purchase was recently completed.
